Personal Profile

My name is Marco Kleimans and as of today, I am . I am currently living in Houston, Texas, with my amazing girlfriend who is finishing her Master's degree at Rice University. I was born in Moscow, Russia, but spent most of my childhood in Buenos Aires, Argentina, where I grew up. I then moved to Los Angeles, California, where I graduated from the University of Southern California with a Bachelor's and a Master's degree in Environmental Engineering. After graduation – and as a result of life decisions and opportunities – I decided to pivot away from Environmental Engineering and started working as a Web Developer at the Information Sciences Institute.

As Jostein Gaarder claims - my favorite writer and philosopher from Norway - embracing uncertainty and accepting one's shortcomings is a critical first step towards self-improvement and realization. His book "The Solitaire Mystery" has been a hallmark for me, as it narrates the story of a father and son who relentlessly search for their missing wife and mother, while strange encounters with mythical creatures along the way inspire a though-provoking exploration of the meaning of life. This is, without doubt, a metaphor of meaning, of reaching the unthinkable, and being able to enjoy the journey while welcoming the unexpected.

This story is my banner: I always enthusiastic about facing new challenges that force me to step outside my comfort zone, but I am also pragmatic and always looking forward to learning and developing new skills. I consider myself methodical and organized, persevering and energetic, and both flexible and always well-disposed. I'm passionate about new technologies, and optimistic about the future. Having a multicultural background allows me to analyze complex situations from different perspectives to find the most fitting solution for each scenario. I enjoy learning, sharing, and helping others accomplish their goals, approaching all tasks with open-mindedness and excitement.
